# Blytz.Auto Product Framing

> Last updated: 2026-04-08

## Summary

Blytz.Auto is a workshop operating SaaS for **predictable-duration automotive services**.

The MVP is intentionally narrow. It is **not** a full workshop ERP and **not** a marketplace.

## Target Customers

Start with workshops that can operate on predictable booking durations:

- car tint shops
- detailing shops
- car wash operators
- aircond/basic service shops

Avoid general-repair complexity in MVP.

## Core Promise

Help a workshop owner manage:

- bookings
- customers
- vehicles
- jobs/status
- service packages

in one web app, without WhatsApp-only operational chaos.

## Business Model

Blytz.Auto is a **subscription SaaS**.

- workshops pay Blytz.Auto
- customers pay the workshop directly
- no marketplace payout or disbursement logic in MVP

## What Blytz.Auto Is Not Yet

- not a full automotive ERP
- not inventory/accounting software
- not multi-branch enterprise software
- not a customer payment marketplace
- not a per-tenant isolated infra platform in MVP

## Product Principles

1. keep the product operationally simple for a solo founder
2. choose boring, maintainable architecture over impressive infra
3. secure tenant data with application-level isolation first
4. support real bookings reliably before adding advanced automation
5. prefer features that directly help workshops run daily operations
